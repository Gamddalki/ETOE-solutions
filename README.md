# ETOE Solutions Co., Ltd.
Freelance Web Development Project
- Category: Web & Mobile
- Roles: Developer, UI/UX Designer
- Tools: JavaScript, React, Node.js / Pug, SCSS

## From Legacy Structure to Evolving Identity
— revitalizing a corporate web presence through continuous modernization and long-term strategic partnership.

### ⚙️ Motivation
ETOE Solutions Co., Ltd., an Oracle MP (Managed Partner) Company, required a webpage that matched their professional standing. The original website was built on legacy PHP, which had become visually outdated and difficult to maintain.
<img width="6188" height="1162" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bb3aa2ad-e831-439d-a5ac-895277e85db2" />
The primary objective was not just a one-time fix, but a long-term modernization strategy. This involved migrating the infrastructure to a modern stack and executing two major design renewals (in 2022 and 2025) to accommodate the company’s expanding business scope and evolving brand identity.

### ⚙️ Approach
As the sole developer and designer, I managed the entire lifecycle of the company’s web presence over several years.
- **Phase 1 (2022-02 ~ 2022-08)**: I migrated the existing PHP website to a Node.js architecture utilizing Pug and SCSS. This phase focused on performance optimization and establishing a responsive, component-based structure that would be easy to maintain. A pivotal improvement during this phase was transitioning the infrastructure to container-based hosting.
- **Maintenance & Phase 2 (2025-06 ~ 2025-12)**: After providing stable maintenance for three years, I led a second major renewal using React. This phase involved a complete UI/UX overhaul to reflect modern design trends and the development of specific pages tailored to new client requirements.
<img width="1800" height="650" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1c5ac5c4-7d6c-4f91-bd7d-84095328ce0f" />
Throughout the process, I focused on clarity and professionalism, ensuring that the company’s services and Oracle partnership status were presented effectively.

### ⚙️ Outcomes
- **Reduced monthly operational costs by 80%** and improved scalability by migrating hosting infrastructure to a container-based environment.
- Transformed an outdated PHP site into a modern, responsive Single Page Application (SPA) using React.
- Delivered two major version upgrades over a 3-year span, proving reliability and consistent quality.
- Designed a clean, professional UI/UX that aligns with the corporate identity of an IT solution provider.

### ⚙️ Reflection
This project highlights the value of long-term client relationships and the ability to evolve a product over time. Moving beyond a simple “build and deliver” model, I acted as a continuous partner for ETOE Solutions. It demonstrated my capability to not only execute technical migrations but also to revisit and improve my own work years later, adapting to new design trends and business needs while maintaining technical stability.
